Wave, bof, Buzz, pas terrible, qui utilise ces deux outils de Google ? Pas moi en tout cas. Un système relationnel construit à partir de mon carnet d'adresse ne me séduit pas du tout. Que faire alors, pour contrer l'ogre FaceBook ? La réponse serait dans les laboratoires de chez Google.

Pour suivre cette rumeur, il faut se rendre à l'adresse : Is "Google Me" a fake rumor? Misleading evolutionary product update? Or is it really a new social network from Google?.

Adam D'Angelo y révele quelques secrets :

  • This is not a rumor. This is a real project. There are a large number of people working on it. I am completely confident about this.
  • They realized that Buzz wasn’t enough and that they need to build out a full, first-class social network. They are modeling it off of Facebook.
  • Unlike previous attempts (before Buzz at least), this is a high-priority project within Google.
  • They had assumed that Facebook’s growth would slow as it grew, and that Facebook wouldn’t be able to have too much leverage over them, but then it just didn’t stop, and now they are really scared.

Google a peur, peur de FaceBook, ils doivent réagir et pour cela miser sur l'innovation dans la grosse artillerie.
